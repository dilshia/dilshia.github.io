Tips For Beautiful Food Photography

  1. Shoot in natural light
  2. Take control of the shadows
  3. Use a neutral background
  4. Colors have a big impact on your composition and they can affect the overall feel of the image
  5. Shooting from above is often the best angle
  6. Arrange your food neatly
  7. Consider leaving some breathing space around the plate so that it doesn't fill the entire frame
  8. Decorate the scene - the food and background are both vital elements
  9. Add a human element to the photos (e.g. by adding a hand or arm in the food pictures)
  10. Keep it simple!
