Zeret Kitchen

A family run restaurant established in 2004, with a strong commitment to authenticity, freshness and quality. They source, cook and serve traditional Ethiopian food with the highest degree of integrity. They cater to the palates of both meat eaters and vegetarians.

Cuisine - African, Ethiopian

Their menu accommodates all palates and is very flavoursome.

Cuisine - Mediterranean, Moroccan
