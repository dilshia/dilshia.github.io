Liman Restaurant

The food is simply splendid; try the special kebabs, vegetarian and fish dishes– with an emphasis on fresh salads. For the ultimate treat, try the exquisite home made baklava.

Cuisine - Mediterranean, Turkish

The Clink Restaurant

A unique dining experience within the walls of a Category C prison. The delicious meals are cooked using fresh, seasonal ingredients and you will be served by prisoners participating in The Clink Restaurant training scheme.

Cuisine - British

YUU Kitchen Restaurant

Yuu Kitchen is inspired by the fun, and freshness of flavours of South-East Asia and the Pacific Rim. Fill your table with bao buns, bursting with buttermilk chicken, crispy tofu or 7UP-braised pork belly. Savour a poke bowl or salmon and jalapeño tostadas from the raw section. Devour zingy grilled octopus and green papaya salad with some gyoza.

Cuisine - Asian, Japanese
